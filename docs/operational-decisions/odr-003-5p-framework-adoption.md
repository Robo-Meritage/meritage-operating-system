# ODR-003: Why Meritage Adopted the 5P Framework

**Date:** 2026-07-01  
**Status:** Active  
**Superseded by:** N/A

---

## Decision

Meritage evaluates all prospective and active engagements through five structured lenses: People, Performance, Position, Potential, and Preparedness. This framework is applied consistently across qualification, valuation, positioning, buyer outreach, management calls, and due diligence.

---

## Context

Before the 5P Framework, Meritage's evaluation of prospective clients was largely unstructured. Different advisors weighted different factors. Financial metrics dominated some assessments while operational factors dominated others. There was no shared vocabulary for communicating why a business was strong or where it had risk.

This created three problems:

1. **Inconsistency.** Two advisors assessing the same business would reach different conclusions based on different instincts.
2. 2. **Incomplete coverage.** Advisors who were financially oriented would underweight management risk or market position. Those with operational backgrounds might underweight financial quality.
   3. 3. **Poor knowledge transfer.** When an advisor left an engagement or the firm, the reasoning behind prior assessments was lost.
     
      4. The firm needed a repeatable, teachable evaluation structure that could be applied consistently by advisors with different backgrounds.
     
      5. ---
     
      6. ## Options Considered
     
      7. 1. **Unstructured advisor judgment** — Continue relying on individual advisor expertise and instinct. Low overhead, but produces inconsistent results and does not scale.
        
         2. 2. **Financial-only scoring model** — Evaluate businesses primarily on financial metrics (EBITDA margin, revenue growth, concentration ratios). Objective and measurable, but ignores factors that materially affect transaction outcomes (management depth, market position, owner motivation).
           
            3. 3. **Proprietary multi-lens framework (5P)** — Structure the evaluation around five recurring lenses that cover all material dimensions of a lower middle market business. Apply the same lenses at every stage of the engagement lifecycle.
              
               4. 4. **Third-party frameworks (e.g., SWOT, McKinsey 7S)** — Borrow established frameworks from strategy consulting. These are well-known and credible but not designed for sell-side advisory and do not map to the stages of a sale process.
                 
                  5. ---
                 
                  6. ## Rationale
                 
                  7. The 5P Framework was designed specifically for how Meritage evaluates and represents lower middle market businesses:
                 
                  8. - **People** ensures that management depth, ownership structure, and key-person risk are evaluated explicitly rather than assumed.
                     - - **Performance** grounds the evaluation in financial reality — not just current results but the quality, consistency, and credibility of those results.
                       - - **Position** captures competitive advantage and market dynamics that drive buyer interest beyond the financial statements.
                         - - **Potential** addresses the forward-looking thesis that buyers pay for: what does this business become under new ownership?
                           - - **Preparedness** is the dimension most often underweighted by brokers and most often fatal to transactions: Is this business actually ready to go through a sale process?
                            
                             - The five lenses are memorable, teachable, and consistently applicable. A new advisor can learn to use them in days. A buyer can be educated on them in a management call. An AI assistant can apply them with structured prompts.
                            
                             - Perhaps most importantly, the 5P Framework creates a shared language across the firm. When an advisor says “this business has a strong Position but weak Preparedness,” every team member understands exactly what that means.
                            
                             - ---

                             ## Implications

                             - The 5P Framework is applied at qualification, valuation, positioning, buyer outreach, and due diligence.
                             - - All qualification scorecards are organized by 5P lenses.
                               - - Discovery questionnaires are structured by 5P lenses.
                                 - - The Positioning Brief and Investment Thesis reference 5P language.
                                   - - AI prompts for engagement work use 5P structure as context.
                                     - - New advisor onboarding includes 5P Framework training as a foundational skill.

# Buy-Side Engagement Fee Structures

**Source:** Fee Structure Options conversation (May 1, 2026)
**Classification:** Score 4 — Important operational template
**Category:** Agreements / Fee Structures

---

## Context

This document captures the three-tier buy-side engagement fee structure developed for Meritage's buy-side M&A mandates. The template was developed during a conversation with Merlin Law Group (buy-side client).

---

## Three Fee Structure Options

### Option 1 — Full Buy-Side Mandate
Best for: Groups actively building pipeline and expecting to be in market consistently

- **Monthly retainer:** $6,500 to $8,500/month
- **Success fee:** 3% to 5% of Purchase Price
- **Scope:** Ongoing sourcing, outreach, and introduction of qualified opportunities

---

### Option 2 — Phased Mandate (Recommended Default)
Best for: Groups who want to move in the buy-side direction but aren't fully ready yet from a priority or bandwidth standpoint

#### Phase 1 — Strategy and Initial Sourcing
- **Monthly:** $2,000/month
- **Commitment:** Month-to-month, no long-term commitment
- **Scope:**
  - Define acquisition criteria and target buy box
  - Initial market mapping and outreach
  - Identify and qualify early opportunities
  - Build preliminary pipeline

#### Phase 2 — Full Buy-Side Mandate
Triggered when mutually aligned on pursuing a specific opportunity

- **Monthly:** $5,000/month
- **Success fee:** Reverse Lehman Scale
  - 5.0% of the first $1,000,000
  - 4.0% of the next $4,000,000 (up to $5M)
  - 3.0% of the next $5,000,000 (up to $10M)
  - 2.0% of the next $10,000,000 (up to $20M)
  - 1.0% above $20,000,000
- **Full support:** Deal structuring, financial analysis, diligence coordination, buyer/seller communication, execution through closing

---

### Option 3 — Success-Fee Dominant
Best for: Groups who want to engage only when something actionable is directly in front of them

- **Monthly:** No ongoing retainer during sourcing
- **Activation fee:** $7,500 per opportunity decided to pursue
- **Success fee:** 3.5% to 4.5%
- **Nature:** Transactional — only triggered when an opportunity is in active pursuit

---

## Positioning / Decision Framework

*"At a high level, the only real question is how aggressive you want to be over the next 6 to 12 months."*

| Goal | Recommended Option |
|---|---|
| Build pipeline proactively | Option 2 (Phase 1 + 2) |
| Wait until something actionable | Option 3 |
| All-in, fully active | Option 1 |

**Strategic note:** Option 3's primary purpose is often to remove the "we don't want monthly fees" objection and nudge the client toward Option 2. Option 2 is typically the target outcome.

## Original Merlin Proposal (November 2025)
The initial proposal to Merlin Law contained:
- **Option A:** $8,500/month + 3% success fee
- **Option B:** $6,500/month + 5% success fee
- Both structured for active mandate with 18-month tail on introductions

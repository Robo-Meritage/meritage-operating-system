## Non-Standard Fee Schedule (PlanNet)

Andrew flagged a typo/overlap in the fee schedule at the $4M-$5M range. The structure appears to be **descending from 8%** - different from the standard ascending sell-side structure:

| Tier | Rate |
|---|---|
| First $1M | 8% |
| Second $1M | 7% |
| Third $1M | 6% |
| Fourth $1M | 5% |
| Amounts above $4M | 4% |

**CONFLICT TO FLAG:** This descending sell-side structure starting at 8% conflicts with the standard ascending sell-side Reverse Lehman (1/2/3/4/5%) in the fee framework record. This may be a non-standard structure for PlanNet specifically, a different product offering, or a template error.

## Meritage's Three Firm Positions

1. **No buyer carve-out** - Andrew wanted to carve out existing interested buyers (Vela/AlfaTech, Salute). Rejected.
2. **No fee discount** - Andrew said fees were higher than expected. Declined. (Contingency: increase monthly engagement fee in exchange for slightly lower success fee, if needed.)
3. **'Paid when paid' clarification** - Success fee due when client actually receives proceeds.

---

## Buyer Carve-Out Request (Rejected)

Andrew's position: Vela/AlfaTech and Salute told him not to hire an advisor. He wanted them carved out of Meritage's fee. Meritage's position: No carve-out. All buyers in the process are subject to the same success fee.

# Roam Deal Communication Policy

**Source:** Deal Chat Communication conversation (May 14, 2026)
**Classification:** Score 4 — Operational standard
**Category:** Operations / Communications

---

## Policy Overview

Meritage uses **Roam** as its primary team communication platform. As of May 14, 2026, dedicated deal-specific chats were created in Roam for each active transaction.

---

## Core Rule

**Use deal-specific chats for all communication related to a specific transaction.**

Do NOT post deal-specific items in the general Deal Team chat unless:
- It impacts multiple transactions simultaneously
- It requires broader team visibility

---

## What Belongs in Deal-Specific Chats

All of the following must be routed to the appropriate deal chat:
- Buyer and seller updates
- NDA status
- CIM and data room discussions
- Buyer outreach activity
- Internal strategy discussions
- Scheduling and follow-up coordination
- Documents and approvals
- Transaction-specific issues or roadblocks

---

## Purpose / Rationale

This policy exists to:
- Keep deal communication organized
- Reduce missed updates and duplicate conversations
- Maintain a clear historical record for each transaction
- Improve visibility across the team

---

## Roam as Performance Management Tool

The Skinner Attendance Critique conversation (May 13, 2026) revealed that Meritage uses Roam engagement metrics to assess team participation. Key metrics tracked:
- Total meetings in Roam
- Total time in Roam
- Days signed in
- Chats sent
- Meetings attended (in office vs. remote)

Low Roam presence correlates with: lower accessibility, reduced collaboration, lower visibility, reduced team cohesion.

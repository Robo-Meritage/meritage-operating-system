# Gallant Dill Partnership Economics

**Source:** Gallant Proposal Generation conversation (May 15, 2026)
**Classification:** Score 4 — Important refinement, ecosystem partner economics
**Category:** AppleBites / Partner Economics

---

## Overview

Gallant Dill is an operator/influencer with a significant following who serves as a distribution partner for Meritage's ecosystem products. The partnership involves licensing Apple Bites assessments through Gallant's organization and embedding Empire Builder as a nurture pathway.

---

## Partnership Economics (Proposed Structure — May 2026)

### Apple Bites Licensing
- **Licensing Fee:** $10,000/month
- **Revenue Participation:** 3% of Apple Bites assessment revenue sold through Gallant's organization
- **Rights:** Includes white-label/co-branded rights plus backend support

**Assessment:** $10k/month is reasonable IF:
- Lead volume is legitimate
- Apple Bites becomes operational infrastructure for his organization
- The platform materially increases his monetization capability
- 3% is light if platform creates significant value; the fixed overhead is the bigger question depending on operational maturity

### Empire Builder
- **Monthly option:** $397/month (group coaching calls + community access + exit readiness discussions)
- **Annual option:** $6,500/year (coursework + training + coaching access + operational/valuation guidance)
- **Referral participation:** 10% on all Empire Builder enrollments

---

## Ecosystem Flow Through Gallant Channel

The partnership is structured around the seller lifecycle:
1. **Apple Bites** — Assessment and readiness scoring (Gallant introduces clients)
2. **Empire Builder** — Nurture path for businesses not yet ready to sell
3. **Meritage Sell-Side** — Transaction execution when ready

**Key principle:** Empire Builder is the nurture path for businesses not yet ready to sell. It positions them for future engagement rather than losing them from the ecosystem.

---

## Consistency Rule

The 10% referral participation on Empire Builder was chosen to maintain consistency across the partnership structure:
- Apple Bites: revenue participation
- Empire Builder: 10% referral participation
- Sell-side referrals: TBD/separate agreement

Keeping compensation consistent across all products makes the proposal cleaner and easier for the partner to understand operationally.

## Context: CRM Naming (Orchard)

In the same period (May 2026), discussions about the CRM naming identified "Orchard" as the recommended name for the platform that connects all ecosystem products:

**Recommended architecture:**
- Apple Bites Assessment → Orchard CRM → Empire Builder

The "Orchard" name was preferred because:
- Apple Bites naturally feeds into Orchard
- Empire Builder naturally grows from Orchard
- Represents accumulation and cultivation
- Not gimmicky; scalable and sophisticated

**What to avoid:** AppleCore, Seed, Harvest, Treehouse, EmpireOS — too startup-y, too literal, or reduces perceived enterprise value.

**Strategic observation:** Meritage is building a "business owner lifecycle ecosystem":
Assessment → Optimization → Scaling → Exit → Wealth

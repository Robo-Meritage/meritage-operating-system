# Module 10: Letter of Intent

> How Meritage negotiates and executes the LOI with the selected Buyer.
>
> ---
>
> ## Purpose
>
> The Letter of Intent (LOI) is the first binding expression of deal terms between the Seller and a single Buyer. This module covers how Meritage advises on LOI terms, negotiates on behalf of the Seller, and manages the exclusivity period that follows.

> ## Scope
>
> **In scope**
>
> - Solicitation of LOIs from Management Call participants
> - - LOI evaluation and comparison
>   - - LOI negotiation on behalf of Seller
>     - - Execution of LOI and management of exclusivity period
>      
>       - **Out of scope**
>      
>       - - Definitive Agreement negotiation (that occurs in Due Diligence/Closing)
>         - - Legal review of LOI (requires outside counsel)
>          
>           - ---
>
> ## Roles and Responsibilities
>
> | Role | Responsibility |
> |---|---|
> | Principal Advisor | Leads LOI negotiation; advises Seller on terms |
> | Seller | Reviews and executes LOI |
> | Legal Counsel | Reviews LOI for legal adequacy (external engagement) |
> | COO | Tracks LOI terms and exclusivity timeline |
>
> ---
>
> ## Inputs
>
> - Management Call completion and buyer shortlist
> - - Valuation floor and target (from 04-valuation.md)
>   - - Seller's stated deal priorities (price, structure, earnout, transition)

> ## AI Assistance
>
> **AI can assist with:**
>
> - Summarizing and comparing LOI terms across multiple buyers
> - - Drafting LOI comparison matrices
>   - - Identifying LOI terms that warrant further negotiation
>    
>     - **AI should not:**
>    
>     - - Provide legal advice on LOI terms
>       - - Advise the Seller on which LOI to accept
>         - - Draft or modify the LOI itself without advisor review

# Module 02: Qualification

> How Meritage evaluates whether a Seller and their Company are the right fit for an engagement.
>
> ---
>
> ## Purpose
>
> Qualification is the stage where Meritage determines whether an Opportunity is worth pursuing and whether the Seller and their Company meet the criteria for a Meritage engagement. It is also where the Seller evaluates whether Meritage is the right advisor for them.
>

> ## Scope
>
> **In scope**
> - Discovery conversations with potential Sellers
> - - Preliminary financial review
>   - - Assessment of Seller readiness, motivation, and timeline
>     - - Preliminary valuation range discussion
>       - - Decision to pursue or decline the engagement
>        
>         - **Out of scope**
>         - - Formal valuation (see [04-valuation.md](04-valuation.md))
>           - - Engagement Agreement negotiation (see [03-engagement.md](03-engagement.md))
>             - - Deal structuring
>              
>               - ---
>
> ## Roles and Responsibilities
>
> | Role | Responsibility |
> |---|---|
> | Principal Advisor | Leads qualification conversations; makes pursue/decline recommendation |
> | COO | Final approval on non-standard or complex engagements |
>

> ## AI Assistance
>
> **AI can assist with:**
> - Researching the Company and industry before qualification calls
> - - Preparing discovery call question frameworks
>   - - Summarizing preliminary financial information provided by the Seller
>     - - Drafting qualification assessment summaries
>      
>       - **AI should not:**
>       - - Determine whether to pursue an engagement — that is a judgment call requiring advisor experience
>         - - Assess Seller motivations or readiness without advisor interpretation
>           - - Provide a valuation estimate based solely on AI analysis

# Module 12: Closing

> How Meritage coordinates the final steps to execute and close the transaction.
>
> ---
>
> ## Purpose
>
> Closing is the final execution stage — from Definitive Agreement through funds transfer and transaction close. This module covers how Meritage coordinates legal, financial, and operational close activities on behalf of the Seller.

> ## Scope
>
> **In scope**
>
> - Coordination of Definitive Agreement negotiation (with outside counsel)
> - - Closing checklist management
>   - - Seller preparation for closing
>     - - Closing mechanics and funds flow coordination
>      
>       - **Out of scope**
>      
>       - - Legal drafting of Definitive Agreement (requires outside counsel)
>         - - Tax structuring (requires CPA/tax attorney)
>           - - Post-close transition management (see 13-post-close.md)
>            
>             - ---
>
> ## Roles and Responsibilities
>
> | Role | Responsibility |
> |---|---|
> | Principal Advisor | Coordinates closing; protects Seller's interests through Definitive Agreement process |
> | Seller | Executes Definitive Agreement; participates in closing |
> | Legal Counsel (external) | Drafts and negotiates Definitive Agreement |
> | COO | Tracks closing checklist; coordinates logistics |
>

> ## AI Assistance
>
> **AI can assist with:**
>
> - Drafting and tracking closing checklists
> - - Summarizing Definitive Agreement terms for Seller review (not legal advice)
>   - - Drafting closing communications and announcements
>    
>     - **AI should not:**
>    
>     - - Provide legal advice on Definitive Agreement terms
>       - - Execute any closing documents
>         - - Make representations about tax treatment of deal proceeds

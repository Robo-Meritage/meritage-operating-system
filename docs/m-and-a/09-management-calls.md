# Module 09: Management Calls

> How Meritage prepares, facilitates, and follows up on Management Calls with Prospective Buyers.
>
> ---
>
> ## Purpose
>
> Management Calls are the first direct interaction between the Seller and shortlisted Prospective Buyers. This module covers preparation, facilitation, and follow-up — including how to manage Seller presentation, control the narrative, and assess buyer quality based on call behavior.

>

>

> ## Roles and Responsibilities
>
> | Role | Responsibility |
> |---|---|
> | Principal Advisor | Facilitates call; manages narrative; assesses buyer behavior |
> | Seller | Presents the business; answers questions with advisor coaching |
> | COO | Schedules logistics; tracks call notes and buyer signals |

> ## AI Assistance
>
> **AI can assist with:**
>
> - Drafting Management Call preparation materials and talking points
> - - Generating anticipated buyer questions and suggested responses
>   - - Drafting post-call summary notes
>     - - Researching specific buyers before calls
>      
>       - **AI should not:**
>      
>       - - Participate in or facilitate the call itself
>         - - Assess buyer intent or quality — this requires advisor judgment
>           - - Coach the Seller on how to respond to specific legal or financial questions

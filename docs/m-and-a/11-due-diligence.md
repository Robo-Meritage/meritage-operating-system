# Module 11: Due Diligence

> How Meritage manages the due diligence process on behalf of the Seller.
>
> ---
>
> ## Purpose
>
> Due Diligence is the buyer's formal investigation of the Company following LOI execution. This module covers how Meritage organizes, manages, and protects the Seller through the due diligence process — including data room management, information request responses, and quality-of-earnings coordination.

> ## Scope
>
> **In scope**
>
> - Data room preparation and management
> - - Information request tracking and response
>   - - Quality-of-earnings coordination (with CPA)
>     - - Seller coaching during due diligence
>       - - Identification and mitigation of deal risks surfaced during diligence
>        
>         - **Out of scope**
>        
>         - - Legal due diligence (requires outside counsel)
>           - - Financial audit (requires licensed CPA)
>             - - Environmental or technical due diligence (requires specialists)
>              
>               - ---
>
> ## Roles and Responsibilities
>
> | Role | Responsibility |
> |---|---|
> | Principal Advisor | Manages diligence process; protects Seller from overreach; advises on risk mitigation |
> | Seller | Provides requested information; participates in diligence calls |
> | COO | Manages data room; tracks information requests and responses |
> | CPA (external) | Coordinates quality-of-earnings process |
>
> ---
>
> ## Inputs
>
> - Executed LOI with stated exclusivity period
> - - Existing financial documentation
>   - - Preliminary due diligence request list from Buyer

> ## AI Assistance
>
> **AI can assist with:**
>
> - Organizing and tracking due diligence request lists
> - - Drafting data room index and document organization
>   - - Summarizing information requests and flagging unusual or high-risk requests
>     - - Drafting responses to routine informational requests
>      
>       - **AI should not:**
>      
>       - - Make representations about the Company's financial condition
>         - - Respond to legal, tax, or environmental questions without advisor review
>           - - Determine what information to withhold or disclose

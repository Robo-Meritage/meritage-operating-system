# Module 07: Buyer Outreach

> How Meritage contacts, qualifies, and advances Prospective Buyers through the initial outreach phase.
>
> ---
>
> ## Purpose
>
> Buyer Outreach is the first stage of active market engagement. The Company's story reaches the market through targeted, confidential outreach to a curated buyer list. This stage manages NDA execution, Teaser distribution, CIM distribution, and initial buyer response tracking.

> ## Scope
>
> **In scope**
>
> - NDA preparation and execution
> - - Teaser distribution to target buyer list
>   - - CIM distribution to executed NDA holders
>     - - Initial buyer response tracking and qualification
>       - - First-round communication with Prospective Buyers
>        
>         - **Out of scope**
>        
>         - - CIM and Teaser preparation (see 06-gtm.md)
>           - - Management Call scheduling and facilitation (see 09-management-calls.md)
>            
>             - ---
>
> ## Roles and Responsibilities
>
> | Role | Responsibility |
> |---|---|
> | Principal Advisor | Leads buyer outreach; owns buyer relationships and communications |
> | COO | Tracks NDA execution and buyer response pipeline |
> | Seller | Reviews and approves buyer list; informed of outreach progress |

> ## AI Assistance
>
> **AI can assist with:**
>
> - Drafting initial outreach communications based on positioning strategy
> - - Tracking and organizing buyer response data
>   - - Researching individual buyer profiles and investment theses
>     - - Drafting NDA cover communications
>      
>       - **AI should not:**
>      
>       - - Make representations to buyers about the Company's value or performance
>         - - Execute outreach independently without advisor review
>           - - Determine buyer qualification without advisor input

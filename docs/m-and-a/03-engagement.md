—————–——–—→→→→→→→→→→–————–———————–————→→————# Module 03: Engagement

> How Meritage formalizes the client relationship and launches an active deal.
>
> ---
>
> ## Purpose
>
> Engagement is the stage where the Engagement Agreement is signed, Meritage is formally retained, and the Deal is opened. This module covers the onboarding of the Seller, the establishment of timelines and milestones, the collection of deal materials, and the alignment of expectations before deal preparation begins.

> ## AI Assistance
>
> **AI can assist with:**
> - Drafting Seller onboarding communications and document request lists
> - - Summarizing Engagement Agreement terms for Seller review
>   - - Preparing initial deal timeline templates
>     - - Organizing and labeling financial documents provided by the Seller
>      
>       - **AI should not:**
>       - - Negotiate Engagement Agreement terms on behalf of Meritage
>         - - Advise the Seller on whether the agreement terms are acceptable to them
>           - - Make commitments about fees, timeline, or deliverables without advisor approval

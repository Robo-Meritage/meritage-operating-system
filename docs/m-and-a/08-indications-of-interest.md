# Module 08: Indications of Interest

> How Meritage solicits, evaluates, and advances Indications of Interest from Prospective Buyers.
>
> ---
>
> ## Purpose
>
> Indications of Interest (IOIs) are non-binding expressions of buyer intent submitted in response to the CIM. This module covers how Meritage solicits, evaluates, and responds to IOIs — including how to assess IOI quality, communicate results to the Seller, and select which buyers advance to Management Calls.

> ## Roles and Responsibilities
>
> | Role | Responsibility |
> |---|---|
> | Principal Advisor | Evaluates IOIs; advises Seller on selection; manages buyer communication |
> | Seller | Reviews IOIs with advisor; approves buyer shortlist |
> | COO | Tracks IOI receipt, terms, and buyer advancement |

> ## AI Assistance
>
> **AI can assist with:**
>
> - Summarizing and comparing IOI terms across multiple buyers
> - - Drafting IOI evaluation matrices
>   - - Drafting buyer advancement and declination communications
>    
>     - **AI should not:**
>    
>     - - Evaluate or rank IOIs without advisor review
>       - - Advise the Seller on which IOI to accept
>         - - Communicate with buyers independently

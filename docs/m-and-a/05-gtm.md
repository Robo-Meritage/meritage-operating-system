# Module 05: Go-to-Market

> How Meritage prepares a Company for the buyer market.
>
> ---
>
> ## Purpose
>
> Go-to-Market (GTM) is the preparation stage that transforms a qualified, engaged Company into a market-ready deal. It produces the Teaser, the CIM, and the target buyer list. The Company is not yet being presented to buyers during this stage. GTM ends when all materials are Seller-approved and outreach is ready to begin.

> ## AI Assistance
>
> **AI can assist with:**
> - Drafting Teaser and CIM sections based on Seller-provided information
> - - Researching target buyer profiles and buyer universe
>   - - Formatting financial exhibits for the CIM
>     - - Proofreading and consistency-checking CIM drafts
>       - - Drafting the Company's investment thesis narrative
>        
>         - **AI should not:**
>         - - Determine deal positioning strategy without advisor direction
>           - - Finalize the buyer list without advisor review and Seller input
>             - - Approve CIM content — that requires Seller sign-off
>               - - Make claims about the Company's future performance

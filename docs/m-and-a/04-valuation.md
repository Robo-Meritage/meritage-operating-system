# Module 04: Valuation

> How Meritage determines and communicates a Company's Enterprise Value range.
>
> ---
>
> ## Purpose
>
> Valuation establishes the estimated Enterprise Value range for the Company. This informs deal positioning, Seller expectations, and the floor price for the engagement. The Meritage valuation is an advisory estimate, not a certified appraisal. It provides the analytical foundation for GTM positioning and the basis for evaluating offers when they arrive.

> ## Scope
>
> **In scope**
> - Financial normalization (EBITDA adjustments)
> - - Comparable transaction analysis
>   - - Valuation range development
>     - - Communication of valuation to Seller
>       - - Alignment on deal floor price and expectations
>        
>         - **Out of scope**
>         - - Certified business appraisal (requires a licensed appraiser)
>           - - Tax or legal opinions of value
>             - - Post-LOI valuation disputes
>

> ## AI Assistance
>
> **AI can assist with:**
> - Researching comparable transactions and industry multiples
> - - Formatting and organizing financial normalization schedules
>   - - Drafting valuation summary narratives for Seller presentations
>     - - Summarizing EBITDA bridge calculations
>      
>       - **AI should not:**
>       - - Determine the valuation range — this requires advisor judgment and market knowledge
>         - - Advise the Seller on whether an offered price is acceptable
>           - - Interpret quality-of-earnings findings without advisor review
>             - - Represent the valuation as a certified appraisal

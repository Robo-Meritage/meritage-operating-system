# Module 11: Closing

> How Meritage coordinates the final steps to execute and fund a transaction.
>
> ---
>
> ## Purpose
>
> Closing is the stage where all transaction documents are finalized, closing conditions are satisfied, and consideration is transferred from Buyer to Seller. The Deal is complete when the Purchase Agreement is executed and funds are received. Meritage coordinates the closing process on behalf of the Seller, working alongside legal counsel and the Buyer's team to ensure a clean, timely close.

> ## Scope
>
> **In scope**
> - Closing checklist management
> - - Coordination of Purchase Agreement execution
>   - - Satisfaction of closing conditions
>     - - Funds transfer confirmation
>       - - Success Fee invoicing and collection
>         - - Revenue Share calculation and notification to Affiliate (if applicable)
>          
>           - **Out of scope**
>           - - Legal drafting of Purchase Agreement (external counsel)
>             - - Post-close integration or transition activities (see [12-post-close.md](12-post-close.md))

> ## Roles and Responsibilities
>
> | Role | Responsibility |
> |---|---|
> | Principal Advisor | Manages closing checklist; coordinates between parties; confirms close |
> | External Legal Counsel | Finalizes Purchase Agreement; manages legal closing mechanics |
> | Seller | Executes Purchase Agreement; receives consideration |
> | Buyer | Executes Purchase Agreement; transfers consideration |
> | COO | Approves Success Fee invoice; notifies Affiliate of Revenue Share |

> ## AI Assistance
>
> **AI can assist with:**
> - Tracking closing checklist item status
> - - Drafting closing communications and confirmations
>   - - Calculating Success Fee based on final transaction value
>     - - Preparing Revenue Share calculation for Affiliate notification
>      
>       - **AI should not:**
>       - - Review or advise on Purchase Agreement terms without legal counsel
>         - - Confirm whether closing conditions have been legally satisfied
>           - - Execute any closing document or communication on behalf of Meritage
>             - - Interpret representation and warranty provisions

# Module 07: Indications of Interest

> How Meritage solicits, evaluates, and acts on IOIs from Buyers.
>
> ---
>
> ## Purpose
>
> The Indications of Interest stage begins after Management Calls and focuses on receiving, analyzing, and acting on non-binding offers from Buyers. Meritage advises the Seller on which IOIs to advance, which Buyers to encourage to improve their offers, and which Buyers to decline. This stage ends when the Seller selects a preferred Buyer (or a shortlist) to pursue to LOI.

> ## Scope
>
> **In scope**
> - IOI solicitation and deadline management
> - - IOI analysis and comparison (price, structure, terms, buyer quality)
>   - - Advisory on which Buyers to advance
>     - - Communication to Buyers (advancing, declining, requesting improvement)
>       - - Seller decision support
>        
>         - **Out of scope**
>         - - LOI negotiation (see [09-letter-of-intent.md](09-letter-of-intent.md))
>           - - Legal review of IOI terms
>

> ## Roles and Responsibilities
>
> | Role | Responsibility |
> |---|---|
> | Principal Advisor | Solicits and analyzes IOIs; advises Seller on selection |
> | Seller | Makes final decision on which Buyers to advance |
> | COO | Reviews IOI analysis and recommendation before Seller communication |

> ## AI Assistance
>
> **AI can assist with:**
> - Formatting IOI comparison matrices (price, structure, earnout terms, exclusivity)
> - - Drafting IOI solicitation letters and follow-up communications
>   - - Summarizing buyer profiles and known deal history
>     - - Preparing IOI analysis summaries for Seller review
>      
>       - **AI should not:**
>       - - Recommend which Buyer to select — that requires advisor judgment and knowledge of Seller priorities
>         - - Communicate Buyer decisions (advancing or declining) without advisor approval
>           - - Interpret IOI legal language without legal counsel review

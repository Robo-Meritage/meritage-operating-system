# Module 08: Management Calls

> How Meritage prepares, facilitates, and follows up on the first meeting between a Buyer and the Seller's management team.
>
> ---
>
> ## Purpose
>
> The Management Call is the first substantive meeting between a qualified Buyer and the Seller's management team. It is one of the highest-leverage moments in the transaction process: it either builds conviction in the Buyer or erodes it. Meritage sets the agenda, prepares the Seller, facilitates the call, and manages follow-up. A successful Management Call typically results in an IOI.

>
> | Role | Responsibility |

> ## AI Assistance
>
> **AI can assist with:**
> - Researching the Buyer's background, investment thesis, and prior acquisitions
> - - Drafting Management Call agendas
>   - - Preparing Seller briefing documents (likely questions, positioning notes)
>     - - Drafting post-call follow-up emails to Buyers
>       - - Summarizing call notes and action items
>        
>         - **AI should not:**
>         - - Participate in or represent Meritage on the Management Call
>           - - Assess whether a Buyer is serious without advisor judgment
>             - - Advise the Seller on how to answer specific questions without advisor review

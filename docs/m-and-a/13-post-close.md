# Module 13: Post-Close

> How Meritage supports the Seller through the post-close transition and earnout monitoring period.
>
> ---
>
> ## Purpose
>
> Post-Close covers the period following transaction close. Meritage's role shifts from transaction execution to transition support and, where applicable, earnout monitoring. This module documents how Meritage fulfills its obligations to the Seller after the deal closes.

> ## Scope
>
> **In scope**
>
> - Post-close Seller debrief and transition support
> - - Earnout monitoring and reporting (where applicable)
>   - - Referral network follow-up and relationship maintenance
>     - - Engagement retrospective and lessons learned capture
>      
>       - **Out of scope**
>      
>       - - Post-close legal disputes (requires outside counsel)
>         - - Buyer integration management (buyer's responsibility)
>          
>           - ---
>
> ## Roles and Responsibilities
>
> | Role | Responsibility |
> |---|---|
> | Principal Advisor | Provides post-close support; monitors earnout terms; maintains Seller relationship |
> | COO | Tracks earnout milestones and reporting obligations |
> | Seller | Participates in transition; provides information for earnout tracking |
>

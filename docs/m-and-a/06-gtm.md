# Module 06: Go-to-Market

> How Meritage prepares a Company for the buyer market.
>
> ---
>
> ## Purpose
>
> Go-to-Market (GTM) is the preparation stage that transforms a qualified, engaged, positioned Company into a market-ready deal. It produces the Teaser, the CIM, and the target buyer list. The Company is not yet being presented to buyers during this stage. GTM ends when all materials are Seller-approved and outreach is ready to begin.

> ## Roles and Responsibilities
>
> | Role | Responsibility |
> |---|---|
> | Principal Advisor | Owns GTM preparation; leads material development |
> | Seller | Reviews and approves Teaser, CIM, and buyer list |
> | COO | Reviews materials for quality and consistency before distribution |

> ## AI Assistance
>
> **AI can assist with:**
>
> - Drafting Teaser and CIM sections based on Seller-provided information and the positioning strategy
> - - Researching target buyer profiles and buyer universe
>   - - Formatting financial exhibits for the CIM
>     - - Proofreading and consistency-checking CIM drafts
>      
>       - **AI should not:**
>      
>       - - Determine deal positioning strategy without advisor direction (that belongs in Module 05)
>         - - Finalize the buyer list without advisor review and Seller input
>           - - Approve CIM content — that requires Seller sign-off
>             - - Make claims about the Company's future performance

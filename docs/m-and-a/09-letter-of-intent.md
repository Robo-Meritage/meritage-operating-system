# Module 09: Letter of Intent

> How Meritage negotiates and executes the LOI with the selected Buyer.
>
> ---
>
> ## Purpose
>
> The Letter of Intent stage covers the negotiation and execution of the LOI between the Seller and the selected Buyer. The LOI establishes the agreed price, structure, exclusivity period, and key conditions. It is semi-binding: most provisions are non-binding, but exclusivity and confidentiality are enforceable. LOI execution triggers the Due Diligence period.

> ## Scope
>
> **In scope**
> - LOI request and receipt from selected Buyer
> - - LOI review and analysis on behalf of the Seller
>   - - Negotiation of price, structure, exclusivity period, and conditions
>     - - Coordination with legal counsel
>       - - LOI execution by both parties
>        
>         - **Out of scope**
>         - - Due diligence process (see [10-due-diligence.md](10-due-diligence.md))
>           - - Definitive agreement negotiation

> ## AI Assistance
>
> **AI can assist with:**
> - Summarizing LOI terms in plain language for Seller review
> - - Comparing LOI terms to the original IOI (price, structure changes)
>   - - Drafting negotiation position summaries for advisor use
>     - - Researching standard exclusivity periods and representations in LMM transactions
>      
>       - **AI should not:**
>       - - Interpret LOI legal language or advise on enforceability without legal counsel
>         - - Make negotiation recommendations without advisor review
>           - - Communicate with the Buyer's counsel on LOI terms
>             - - Advise the Seller on whether to accept or reject the LOI without advisor input

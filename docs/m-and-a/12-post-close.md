# Module 12: Post-Close

> How Meritage closes out an engagement, captures lessons learned, and maintains the client relationship.
>
> ---
>
> ## Purpose
>
> Post-Close covers everything that happens after consideration is transferred. Meritage's active advisory role has ended, but the engagement is not truly complete until internal records are closed, fees are collected, Affiliates are notified, and the relationship with the former Seller is properly transitioned. This is also when institutional knowledge from the engagement is captured in MOS.

> ## Scope
>
> **In scope**
> - Success Fee invoicing and collection
> - - Revenue Share payment to Affiliate (if applicable)
>   - - Post-close client communication
>     - - Deal record completion in CRM
>       - - Post-engagement retrospective and lessons learned documentation
>         - - Transition of relationship to post-close status
>          
>           - **Out of scope**
>           - - Earnout monitoring (see lifecycle.md — Earnout Monitoring stage)
>             - - Post-close operational consulting (separate engagement)
>              
>               - ---
>
> ## Roles and Responsibilities
>
> | Role | Responsibility |
> |---|---|
> | Principal Advisor | Closes deal record; sends post-close communication; leads retrospective |
> | COO | Confirms fee collection; approves Revenue Share payment; reviews retrospective |
> | Seller | Receives post-close communication; provides retrospective feedback if requested |
>

> ## AI Assistance
>
> **AI can assist with:**
> - Drafting post-close client communications
> - - Preparing post-engagement retrospective summaries
>   - - Drafting lessons-learned entries for MOS
>     - - Formatting deal record summaries for CRM entry
>      
>       - **AI should not:**
>       - - Calculate or confirm final fee amounts without advisor review of deal terms
>         - - Communicate with the Seller or Buyer post-close without advisor approval
>           - - Make decisions about what lessons to document — that requires advisor reflection

# Module 06: Buyer Outreach

> How Meritage contacts, qualifies, and advances Prospective Buyers.
>
> ---
>
> ## Purpose
>
> Buyer Outreach is the stage where Meritage actively presents the opportunity to qualified Prospective Buyers. Teasers are sent, interest is assessed, NDAs are executed, and CIMs are distributed to signed buyers. The goal is to generate a qualified pool of serious buyers while protecting the Seller's confidentiality.

> ## AI Assistance
>
> **AI can assist with:**
> - Drafting and personalizing Teaser outreach emails
> - - Researching individual buyer profiles and investment criteria
>   - - Tracking buyer responses and summarizing interest levels
>     - - Preparing NDA follow-up communications
>      
>       - **AI should not:**
>       - - Qualify buyer financial capability without advisor review
>         - - Distribute the CIM — NDA execution must be confirmed by a human before distribution
>           - - Approve or exclude buyers from the process without advisor and Seller input
>             - - Represent deal terms or valuations to buyers

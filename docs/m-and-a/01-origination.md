# Module 01: Origination

> How Meritage identifies and develops new sell-side opportunities.
>
> ---
>
> ## Purpose
>
> Origination is the first stage of the Meritage Method. Its purpose is to build and maintain a pipeline of qualified sell-side opportunities through proactive outreach, referral network development, and inbound relationship management.

> ## Scope
>
> **In scope**
> - Direct outreach to potential Sellers
> - - Management of Referral Partner and Affiliate relationships
>   - - Inbound lead qualification
>     - - Pipeline tracking from first contact through Qualification
>      
>       - **Out of scope**
>       - - Qualification conversations (see [02-qualification.md](02-qualification.md))
>         - - Engagement Agreement negotiation (see [03-engagement.md](03-engagement.md))

> ## Roles and Responsibilities
>
> | Role | Responsibility |
> |---|---|
> | Principal Advisor | Owns origination activity and pipeline development |
> | COO | Reviews pipeline; approves pursuit of non-standard opportunities |
> | Referral Partner / Affiliate | Introduces potential Sellers via Qualified Introduction |

> ## AI Assistance
>
> **AI can assist with:**
> - Researching potential Seller targets (industry, revenue signals, ownership)
> - - Drafting outreach messages and follow-up sequences
>   - - Summarizing news or market events relevant to target sectors
>     - - Preparing background briefs on companies before outreach calls
>      
>       - **AI should not:**
>       - - Determine which companies are worth pursuing without advisor judgment
>         - - Send outreach on behalf of Meritage without human review and approval
>           - - Represent Meritage in any communication without explicit authorization

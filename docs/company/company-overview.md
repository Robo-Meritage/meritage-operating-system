# Company Overview

> Meritage Acquisitions — who we are, what we do, and how we operate.
>
> ---
>
> ## Who We Are
>
> Meritage Acquisitions is a lower-middle-market M&A advisory firm specializing in the acquisition and growth of service-oriented businesses. We work alongside founders, operators, and investors to identify, structure, and close transactions that create long-term value.
>
> We are not a traditional investment bank. We are operators who advise — and in many cases, co-invest alongside our clients. Our credibility comes from having done the work ourselves.
>
> ---
>
> ## What We Do
>
> Meritage provides advisory and consulting services across three primary areas:
>
> **M&A Advisory**
> We guide business owners through the sale process from initial valuation through close. This includes positioning, buyer outreach, deal structuring, due diligence support, and negotiation. Our focus is the $1M–$20M enterprise value range where founders often lack access to institutional-quality guidance.
>
> **Transaction Consulting**
> We advise buyers on acquisition strategy, deal sourcing, financial modeling, and integration planning. Engagements are typically retainer-based or project-scoped.
>
> **Operational Consulting (via Robosky Consulting)**
> Through our Robosky Consulting arm, we deploy operational playbooks into businesses before, during, and after transactions — helping management teams install systems, metrics, and decision frameworks that increase enterprise value.
>
> ---
>
> ## Our Market
>
> We operate in the lower-middle market, which we define as businesses with:
>
> - Revenue between $2M and $30M
> - - EBITDA between $500K and $5M
>   - - Enterprise value between $1M and $20M
>     - - Ownership structures that are founder-led or family-owned
>      
>       - This segment is underserved by large investment banks and overcharged by local business brokers. We fill that gap with institutional-quality process and founder-friendly economics.
>      
>       - ---
>
> ## Our Fee Philosophy
>
> We use a modified Reverse Lehman fee structure for sell-side engagements. Unlike the traditional Lehman Formula (which pays higher percentages on smaller deal values), our structure is designed to:
>
> - Align our incentives with maximizing transaction value
> - - Reward effort on larger, more complex transactions
>   - - Remain competitive and transparent with clients
>    
>     - See `docs/m-and-a/reverse-lehman-formula.md` for the full structure.

> ## Our Values
>
> **Operators first.** We understand the weight of selling something you built. We don't treat transactions as paperwork — we treat them as life events.
>
> **Transparency over polish.** We tell clients the truth about their business, their valuation, and their options — even when it's uncomfortable.
>
> **Simplicity.** We favor clear language, simple structures, and direct communication. Complexity is rarely in the client's interest.
>
> **Long-term relationships.** A successful transaction is the beginning of a relationship, not the end of one.
